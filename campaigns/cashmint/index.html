About CashMint

CashMint makes it simple for anyone to create, manage, and broadcast CashTokens on Bitcoin Cash — no coding required. Under the hood it is powered by MintCore, a fully-featured TypeScript SDK with multi-provider UTXO handling, BCMR metadata support, dynamic fee estimation, and a 62-test suite.

Whether you want to launch a fungible token, mint NFTs with mutable or minting capabilities, or attach on-chain BCMR metadata, CashMint has you covered.

MintCore is open-source (MIT) and already production-ready. Your pledge directly funds the web UI, post-genesis operations, CLI tooling, and SLP migration helpers described in the milestones below.
